# 20_DOMAINS — ongoing roles (no deadline)

> The hats you wear that don't end: a job, a side business, a craft, a community you run.
> Each domain is its own folder with its own `CLAUDE.md` holding domain-specific rules.
>
> 끝나지 않는 역할들: 직업, 사이드 비즈니스, 취미·기예, 운영하는 커뮤니티. 각 도메인은
> 자체 `CLAUDE.md`를 가진 폴더.

## Domains

| Folder | What it covers | Entry file |
|---|---|---|
| _DOMAIN_TEMPLATE/ | (template — copy me) | `CLAUDE.md` |

## When to use this vs. others

| Situation | Goes to |
|---|---|
| Has a deadline, will end | `10_WORK/` |
| Ongoing role, no end date | **here** |
| Purely personal (money, family, health) | `50_LIFE/` |

To add a domain: copy `_DOMAIN_TEMPLATE/`, rename it, edit its `CLAUDE.md`.
