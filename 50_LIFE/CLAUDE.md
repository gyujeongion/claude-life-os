## Rules

- **L1 is off here.** Personal-life advice is never governed by public/brand principles.
  (See `00_SELF/context_router.md` — contexts C/D/E/F.)
- Keep truly sensitive numbers and documents out of any repo you make public. Use the
  `.gitignore` block, or keep this folder local-only.
- No secrets/passwords in plain `.md`.
- Health and finance are `[OWNER]`-truth domains: record what the owner states, flag
  inferences clearly, never present a guess as a fact.
